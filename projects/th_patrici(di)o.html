PATRICI(di)O

My very first collaboration with theater. Learning was what I did the most on this project. Music varied a lot, and in most cases, should be heard in live while listening to the actors, otherwise it cannot make any sense at all. Only a few tracks are more suitable to listen separately. My goal was that music could complement that divine space that the play suggests. The plot is about Patricio Aylwin -passed away since 2016- first elected president of Chile post-dictatorship. A very sensitive theme, but told in a very humorously and smart way. It was a pretty fun play to watch from the musician bench actually.
