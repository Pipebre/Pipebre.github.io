This theatre play has an especial place on my heart because how amazingly rich the process of it was. While I was searching the sounds that will accompany the actors on scene, I had the opportunity to learn a lot of myself. A very introspective process indeed. From my vision, it was a very sensitive and fragile work. Aesthetically speaking, the nocturnal elements in conjuntion with nature and an intense sense of search from the characters involved, played an important role on deciding how the music of "Nocturnal Phenomena" (translated) would sound. The central plot of the play dialogues with themes like friendship, conservative institutions and sexuality.
