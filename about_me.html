About me

My name is Felipe Saravia, I'm 23 years old, I'm from Santiago de Chile and I'm finishing my studies in musical composition for audiovisual media. In parallel to my studies, I have been working in little projects whenever I can. I mainly play piano and synthesizers but these days I'm having a lot of fun learning guitar, singing and messing around with the unlimited power of technology.

I'm currently exploring different areas in where to develop myself, like music for videogames, films, theater, pop music, "classical" music, etc. To this day I can't decide one over the other. I love the infinity array of possible scenarios that the entertainment industry offers, where the spectators can freely choose in which and how they want to be part of. I like to be part of that with my voice, which is sound.
